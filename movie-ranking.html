The Best Movies According to Victor

My top 3 movies of all time

Passion for Christ

This is my favourite film, it relates the agony and suffering Jesus Christ had to bear for our sakes, It reminds me that Jesus loved me so much that he was.willing to sacrifice his life and dignity to secure mine

Torchlighters

This is my favourite cartoon series, it shows the documentary of matyrs who were ready to die/suffer for the Kingdom of God to be advanced

Prophet suddenly

This is a popular Nigerian Christian tragedy that teaches a valuable lesson
